THE ADDAMS FAMILY: A NEW MUSICAL

Book by MARSHALL BRICKMAN and RICK ELICE
Music and Lyrics by ANDREW LIPPA
Based on characters created by Charles Addams


An Everett KIDSTAGE TeenSelect Production

Now grown up, Wednesday Addams, the ultimate princess of darkness, has gone and done the unthinkable by falling in love with a sweet young man from a "normal " family.
After her father, Gomez, learns of the tryst, he must do something he’s never done before — keep a secret from his beloved wife, Morticia.
When they inevitably play host to the new beau and his family, get ready for a devilishly grotesque and hilarious dinner party worthy only of the Addams name.
With an original story from Marshall Brickman & Rick Elice (Jersey Boys), wither into the magnificently macabre world of everyone’s favorite First Family of Death in this grisly musical comedy.
