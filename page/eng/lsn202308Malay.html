202308 Malaysia Travel

This time travel in Malaysia was more like being relaxed and chill than being inspirational or studying and learning. Thinking about myself, others, and also, I think I somehow improved my skill in taking pictures with my phone.
After six and a half of flight, my first impression of Malaysia was quite different from what I expected. Unlike Taiwan with the spiking smell of brimstone, Malaysia brought back the memory, joyful heat, and friendly sense of the Philippines when I stayed at the English academy.
Although Malaysia is well-known as a Muslim country, but personally felt that this country isn't forcing me to notice that they are.
What I saw and felt from it was, that aside from how well they naturally put their religious culture into society, I felt this country was the USA within the Asian continent. The way I experienced from the street was that the Chinese, Indians, and Malaysians respected each other and harmonized, instead of fighting and arguing about who was superior.
One surprising thing was that KL before midnight gave me a mood very similar to locals anywhere in South Korea. Not as bright and extremely safe as it is here, but it was not as dangerous as I worried before. After all, we went through several night markets, tour spots, and good food, such a great time, and the nice people with nature made my time way too fast that I realized that I was already on the way back home. For those five days, I made a promise to myself to put aside the work of problems that I carry and take a quality of relaxation. And it might not be one hundred percent what I wanted, which I don't think I will ever consider any travel would do, but I guess I had enough to satisfy.
A travel to Malaysia to chill, and I'm sure I bring back heartwarming and wholesome memories from there.
